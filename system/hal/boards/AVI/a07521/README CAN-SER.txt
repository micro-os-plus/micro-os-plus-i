Multi-purpose CAN/serial board, based on AT90CAN128 and a MAX3243.

Can be configured as: 
- single USART with full RS232 handshake
- double USART without handshake
- BlueTooth module
- LIN bridge

The CAN connector is the standard 10 pin header.

The serials connector is a similar 10 pin header.
